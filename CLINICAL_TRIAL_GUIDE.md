## 2. Mandatory Participant Exclusions

Do **NOT** participate in this 200mg capsule trial if you meet any of the following criteria:

* **Pregnancy or Nursing:** While the trial covers pre-menopause, it is strictly forbidden for pregnant or breastfeeding individuals due to the high uterine-motility effects of concentrated gingerols.
* **Blood Thinning Medications:** Ginger and turmeric possess natural anti-platelet properties. Do not take these capsules if you are on Warfarin, Aspirin, Plavix, or other prescription blood thinners.
* **Upcoming Surgery:** Cease all capsule intake at least 14 days before any scheduled surgical or dental procedure to avoid bleeding risks.
* **Gallstones or Bile Duct Obstruction:** Turmeric/curcumin stimulates gallbladder contractions. If you have active gallstones, this can cause acute severe pain.

## 4. Adverse Event Reporting (What to Do)

If you experience negative side effects during your 14-day cycle, take the following steps:

1. **Stop Immediately:** Cease the protocol. Do not wait for the 14-day mark to finish.
2. **Seek Medical Care:** If symptoms are severe, contact a healthcare professional immediately.
3. **Log the Event:** Open an issue or update your anonymous data log using the `side_effects_reported` column in `data/template.csv`. Accurate safety data is just as valuable to this open-source study as positive data.
