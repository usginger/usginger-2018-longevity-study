# 🧬 Material Transfer Agreement & Research Framework (USG-2018 Roots)

This document establishes the guidelines, terms, and liability parameters under which physical assets, tissue samples, or processed extracts from the **2018 Longevity Study** may be transferred to external research laboratories, commercial sponsors, or academic institutions.

## 1. Scope of Available Physical Assets
* **Baseline Material:** Original 2018 sun-dried/cured agricultural cubes.
* **Extract Profiles:** Metabolic extracts or processed analytical samples derived from the core 2018 batch.

## 2. Intended Use and Compliance
* **Research and Verification:** Samples are provided primarily for independent validation, chemical mapping, metabolic profiling, and academic longevity research.
* **Commercial Integration:** Commercial sponsors looking to integrate or reference this baseline material in commercial product formulations or branding must secure explicit written authorization and comply with active high-tier repository sponsorship agreements.

## 3. Request and Review Process
To initiate a physical asset transfer request, stakeholders must submit a formal inquiry via our contact channels detailing:
1. **Organization Details:** Institutional or corporate affiliation.
2. **Research Intent:** A brief summary of how the 2018 roots will be analyzed or utilized.
3. **Chain of Custody Requirements:** Any specific environmental controls required during shipping and receiving.

## 4. Legal Disclaimers & Liability Protection ("AS-IS" Clauses)
To satisfy corporate compliance and protect the integrity of this open-science initiative, all transfers are subject to the following legal parameters:

* **Experimental Nature:** THE MATERIAL BEING TRANSFERRED IS EXPERIMENTAL IN NATURE. IT IS PROVIDED MERELY AS A BASELINE FOR BIOLOGICAL AND BOTANICAL STABILITY RESEARCH.
* **No Warranties:** THE RECIPIENT ACKNOWLEDGES THAT THE MATERIAL IS PROVIDED "AS IS." THE PROVIDER MAKES NO REPRESENTATIONS AND EXTENDS NO WARRANTIES OF ANY KIND, EITHER EXPRESSED OR IMPLIED, INCLUDING ANY WARRANTY OF MERCHANTABILITY OR FITNESS FOR A PARTICULAR PURPOSE.
* **Assumption of Risk:** The recipient institution assumes all liability for claims for damages against it by third parties which may arise from the recipient's use, storage, or disposal of the physical material.
